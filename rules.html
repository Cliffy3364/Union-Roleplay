OFFICIAL RULEBOOK

Union Roleplay Rules

These rules protect the quality, fairness and enjoyment of roleplay throughout Union City. By playing on the server, you agree to follow every rule listed on this page.

Read the Rules Ask for Help
⚠️

Before You Begin

Not knowing a rule does not excuse breaking it. Rules may be updated as the community develops, so players are responsible for checking this page regularly.

Staff may take action against behaviour that damages roleplay or the community, even where a specific situation is not described word-for-word in this rulebook.

01
SECTION 01

Community & Conduct

Community standards that apply across Union Roleplay and its connected platforms

01.1Respect & InclusionBannable+

Harassment, discrimination, hate speech or slurs targeting any protected characteristic are strictly prohibited.

01.2OOC ConductFailRP+

Out-of-character arguments must be handled through staff tickets only.

01.3Harassment & TargetingBannable+

Repeated targeting of players or groups is considered harassment.

01.4Real-World ThreatsPermanent Ban+

Doxxing, threats or personal attacks result in permanent removal.

01.5Disruptive PlayBannable+

Intentional rule testing or trolling is not allowed.

01.6Rule EncouragementBannable+

Encouraging others to break rules is treated as a violation.

01.7Ban EvasionPermanent Ban+

Returning on alternate accounts after a ban is not permitted.

01.8Age RequirementBannable+

Players must be aged 16 or over. Anyone found to be underage may be removed from the community and banned.

01.9Common Sense Rule3-Strike System+

Staff will judge unclear situations based on realism and intent. Repeated misuse of loopholes may result in strikes.

01.10Real Money TradingPermanent Ban+

Trading in-game assets for real currency outside official systems is prohibited.

01.11Infrastructure AbusePermanent Ban+

Exploiting or damaging server systems results in permanent removal.

01.12External PlatformsBannable+

Behaviour on external platforms must follow Union Roleplay standards.

02
SECTION 02

Roleplay Standards

Core standards for realistic, fair and immersive roleplay

02.1Always In CharacterFailRP+

You must remain in character at all times while connected.

02.2Roleplay QualityFailRP+

Low-effort or unrealistic gameplay is not allowed.

02.3Value of LifeFailRP+

Players must realistically fear for their life when threatened.

02.4New Life RuleFailRP+

Death results in partial memory loss and a cooldown before returning to the scene.

02.5Medical Stages3-Strike System+

Players must respect injury states, including whether they are conscious or unconscious. Abuse of medical states may be handled through strikes.

02.6Combat LoggingBannable+

Disconnecting to avoid consequences is not allowed.

02.7No Breaking CharacterFailRP+

Do not interrupt active roleplay with out-of-character talk.

02.8Sensitive ContentBannable+

Restricted topics such as sexual violence or self-harm are not permitted.

02.9ERPPermanent Ban+

Erotic roleplay is strictly prohibited.

02.10Voice StandardsFailRP+

Players must use a clear and usable microphone.

02.11AFK AbuseFailRP+

Going AFK to avoid consequences is not allowed.

02.12Leadership Standards3-Strike System+

Faction leaders are held to higher expectations and may receive strikes for repeated misconduct.

02.13FearRP WeaponsFailRP+

Players must realistically fear weapons and threats.

02.14Suicide RoleplayBannable+

Players must not create any scene intended to influence or involve suicide.

02.15Custody RoleplayGame Kick+

If you are in custody and handcuffed, you must cooperate and roleplay according to the scenario. Running away in cuffs immediately after being pinned down is FailRP.

03
SECTION 03

In-Game Conduct

Rules governing conflict, scenes, vehicles and player interactions

03.1Random DeathmatchFailRP+

Attacking without valid roleplay is prohibited.

03.2Proper InitiationFailRP+

Hostile actions must be clearly initiated.

03.3Vehicle MisuseFailRP+

Vehicles cannot be used as weapons.

03.4MetagamingBannable+

Using out-of-character information in-game is prohibited.

03.5PowergamingFailRP+

Forcing unrealistic outcomes is not allowed.

03.6BaitingBannable+

Provoking rule breaks is prohibited.

03.7Hostage RulesFailRP+

Hostage roleplay must remain realistic.

03.8Scene InterferenceFailRP+

Do not enter active scenes without a valid reason.

03.9Driving StandardsFailRP+

Driving must reflect realistic UK behaviour.

03.10Returning to SceneFailRP+

Do not return to completed scenes within 30 minutes.

03.11Character SeparationBannable+

Characters cannot share knowledge or assets.

03.12Downed PlayersFailRP+

Downed players are out of the scene.

03.13Rob or Kill RuleFailRP+

You may not both rob and kill unless it is justified by the roleplay.

03.14No Downed RobbingFailRP+

Downed players cannot be robbed.

03.15Vehicle Escapes3-Strike System+

Escapes must be realistic and must not exploit mechanics. Repeated abuse may lead to strikes.

03.16Illegal Placement ExploitsFailRP+

Illegal setups must be realistically accessible.

03.17ScammingFailRP+

Scamming must remain within allowed contexts and limits.

03.18Emergency Scene InterferenceFailRP+

Do not interfere with emergency services.

04.9Cop BaitingFailRP+

04.10Emergency EquipmentBannable+

Misuse or theft of emergency equipment is prohibited.

04.11Police Station ProtectionFailRP+

Hostage roleplay is not permitted inside custody areas.

SECTION 10

Strict Roleplay Enforcement

Additional standards for maintaining serious, believable and consequence-driven roleplay.

10.1Character Consistency3-Strike System+

Your character's behaviour, skills, affiliations and decisions must remain consistent with their established background. Repeatedly changing personality to gain an advantage is prohibited.

10.2Unrealistic EscalationFailRP+

Scenes must escalate naturally. Immediately turning minor disputes into shootings, kidnappings or major pursuits without meaningful roleplay is not permitted.

10.3Consequence AvoidanceBannable+

Using character deletion, asset transfers, disconnecting, job changes or other methods to avoid lawful roleplay consequences is prohibited.

10.4Information DumpingFailRP+

Characters may not instantly reveal extensive criminal, police or personal information without believable knowledge and a valid roleplay reason.

10.5Scene Quality3-Strike System+

Repeatedly creating rushed, repetitive or one-sided scenes that offer no meaningful interaction may result in strikes and role restrictions.

10.6Unrealistic Asset TransfersBannable+

Large transfers of money, vehicles, weapons or property must have a clear in-character reason and may be reviewed by management.

10.7Serial Character AbusePermanent Ban+

Creating disposable characters to harass players, transfer assets, gather information or repeatedly commit rule breaches is prohibited.

10.8Staff Direction During ScenesBannable+

Players must follow reasonable staff directions during active incidents and continue roleplay unless explicitly told the scene is paused.
