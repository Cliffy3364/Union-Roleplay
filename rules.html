Server Rules

These rules are written directly into the Union website so they are clear, searchable and easy to use. Department policies may add further requirements.

Section 01

Community & Respect

Standards that apply in game, Discord, streams and every Union platform

1.1
Respect and inclusionEnforced

Harassment, discrimination, targeted abuse, threats or repeated toxicity are not accepted in Union Roleplay.

1.2
Out-of-character conductStandard

Resolve disagreements calmly through tickets. Public arguments, insults, baiting and attempts to create drama are not permitted.

1.3
Privacy and personal informationSerious

Do not share, request or threaten to expose another person’s private information. Doxxing or credible threats result in immediate action.

1.4
Staff decisions and appealsStandard

Follow reasonable staff instructions during active situations. Decisions may be challenged afterwards through the correct appeal route.

Section 02

Character & Immersion

Keep the world believable and separate player knowledge from character knowledge

2.1
Remain in characterEnforced

Keep active scenes immersive. Use the correct support route for out-of-character issues rather than interrupting roleplay.

2.2
No metagaming or powergamingSerious

Do not use information your character has not learned, force outcomes or use unrealistic mechanics to gain an advantage.

2.3
Value your lifeEnforced

React appropriately to credible danger, injuries and overwhelming force. Characters should not behave as though death or serious harm has no consequence.

2.4
Character separationStandard

Your characters must have separate knowledge, relationships and assets. Do not transfer information or resources simply to benefit another character.

2.5
Realistic names and appearanceStandard

Characters must use believable names and an appearance suitable for the server. Impersonating real public figures or staff is not permitted.

Section 03

Crime & Conflict

Criminal roleplay must produce scenes rather than remove interaction

3.1
Crime must create roleplayEnforced

Criminal activity should generate meaningful scenes. Random violence, low-effort escalation and win-focused behaviour are not permitted.

3.2
Reasonable escalationSerious

Violence must have believable build-up and proportionate cause. Minor insults, traffic incidents or losses do not justify immediate lethal force.

3.3
No random or vehicle deathmatchingSerious

Attacking players or using vehicles as weapons without valid roleplay is prohibited.

3.4
New-life ruleEnforced

After character death, do not return to the same scene or use detailed knowledge of the events leading to death unless staff or an approved system states otherwise.

3.5
Robberies and hostagesStandard

Hostages must be genuine participants, not friends volunteering only to create an advantage. Demands must remain reasonable and support continued roleplay.

3.6
Combat loggingSerious

Do not disconnect, respawn or exploit game mechanics to avoid arrest, injury, loss or an active roleplay situation.

Section 04

Vehicles & Roads

Drive according to the vehicle, road, weather and active scene

4.1
Drive realisticallyEnforced

Use vehicles in a way that fits their type, condition and the scene. Unrealistic stunt driving and vehicle deathmatching are prohibited.

4.2
Vehicle conditionStandard

Treat serious crashes and visible damage appropriately. A badly damaged vehicle should not continue at full performance without repair.

4.3
Emergency vehiclesSerious

Do not steal or misuse marked emergency vehicles without strong roleplay justification and an appropriate scene.

4.4
Aircraft and specialist vehiclesStandard

Use restricted or specialist vehicles only when authorised and capable of operating them realistically.

Section 05

Whitelisted Departments

Extra responsibilities apply to trusted public-service positions

5.1
Emergency service standardsEnforced

Police, medical and other whitelisted roles carry additional departmental responsibilities and must follow their relevant procedures.

5.2
Abuse of positionSerious

Do not use job permissions, equipment, information or access to benefit friends, alternate characters or criminal activity.

5.3
Professional conductStandard

Department members must remain professional, communicate clearly and avoid behaviour that damages public trust or disrupts other players’ scenes.

5.4
Department informationStandard

Sensitive operational information learned through a whitelisted role may not be shared without an in-character reason and proper authority.

Section 06

Economy & Assets

Protect the integrity of progression and the player-driven economy

6.1
No exploiting or duplicationSerious

Do not exploit bugs, duplicate items, manipulate systems or conceal gains caused by an error. Report issues immediately.

6.2
Real-world tradingSerious

Selling or purchasing in-game money, assets, accounts or services outside approved Union systems is prohibited.

6.3
Asset transfersEnforced

Large transfers must have a genuine in-character reason. Moving wealth between your own characters or gifting assets before leaving may be reversed.

6.4
Business roleplayStandard

Player businesses must remain active, fair and roleplay-focused. Ownership does not grant permission to monopolise systems or exclude players without reason.

Section 07

Platform Integrity

Keep the client, voice systems and community platforms fair and secure

7.1
Cheating and unauthorised softwareSerious

Cheats, injected menus, macros that create an unfair advantage, modified hitboxes and unauthorised visual modifications result in permanent removal.

7.2
Bug abuseSerious

Knowingly repeating a bug for advantage is exploitation even if no external software is used.

7.3
Voice and communicationStandard

Use clear, appropriate voice communication. Soundboards, excessive effects and disruptive background audio may be restricted.

7.4
Ban evasionSerious

Attempting to access Union through alternate accounts or identities while sanctioned will extend or make the sanction permanent.
