UNION CITY POLICE DEPARTMENT

Protect & Serve

The Union City Police Department is responsible for protecting the public, investigating crime and maintaining order across the city.

Officers progress through specialist divisions while using advanced British policing systems built exclusively for Union Roleplay.

PLAYER-OWNED BUSINESSES

Build a Brand in Union City

Businesses are a major part of Union Roleplay's player-driven economy. Owners can recruit staff, organise shifts, advertise services, host events and build a reputation across the city.

From restaurants and cafés to dealerships, garages and nightlife venues, every business creates jobs and new roleplay opportunities for the wider community.
