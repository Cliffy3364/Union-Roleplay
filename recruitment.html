More Than Just a Staff Role

Union Roleplay is built by people who care about serious roleplay, community standards and creating a server that players want to return to.

Whether you enjoy helping players, creating vehicles, developing scripts, designing clothing, producing media or organising events, there may be a place for you within the team.

We are looking for dependable people who communicate well, work professionally and are willing to contribute consistently.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Recruitment FAQ

Do I need previous staff experience? +

Previous experience is useful but not essential for every position. Your attitude, judgement, communication and willingness to learn are also important.

Can I apply for more than one position? +

Yes. Select a primary and secondary position. Your application will normally be assessed first for your primary choice.

How long does recruitment take? +

Review times vary depending on the position and number of applications. Suitable applicants will be contacted through Discord.

Will I receive payment? +

Unless specifically stated otherwise, community positions are voluntary. Any paid development work must be agreed separately before work begins.

What happens during the trial period? +

Trial members may receive training, limited permissions and supervised tasks while management evaluates their reliability and performance.
