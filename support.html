BEFORE CONTACTING STAFF

Help Us Resolve Your Issue Faster

Providing the correct information in your first message helps the support team investigate your problem quickly.

01

Explain the issue clearly

Describe what happened, what you expected to happen and when the problem started.

02

Include evidence

Attach screenshots, videos, clips or error messages whenever they are relevant.

03

Provide account details

Include your Discord username, FiveM name and character name where applicable.

04

Remain patient

Do not repeatedly tag staff or open duplicate tickets while waiting for a response.

TROUBLESHOOTING

Common Solutions

Try these steps before opening a technical support ticket.

No support answers matched your search.

FiveM cannot connect to the server +

Confirm that FiveM is running correctly and use the direct server address: 185.223.29.112:30120.

Restart FiveM, check your internet connection and make sure no VPN or firewall is blocking FiveM.

I am stuck on the loading screen +

Close FiveM completely and clear your FiveM cache. Do not delete the game folder or the folder named game.

Restart your computer and reconnect after clearing the cache.

Textures or clothing are missing +

Increase the Extended Texture Budget within the GTA V graphics settings and ensure your graphics drivers are current.

Restart FiveM after changing the setting. Lowering other graphics settings may also help if your graphics memory is limited.

My game keeps crashing +

Verify your GTA V files, update your graphics drivers and remove unsupported graphics modifications.

Take a screenshot of the FiveM crash message before opening a technical ticket.

My microphone does not work in game +

Confirm that the correct input device is selected in Windows, GTA V and FiveM.

Reconnect to the server after changing your microphone settings and check that push-to-talk is correctly bound.

I lost money, items or a vehicle +

Open a support ticket and provide evidence showing what was lost and how the loss occurred.

Compensation is not guaranteed and may only be issued when sufficient evidence is available.

My whitelist role is missing +

Confirm that you are using the same Discord account that was approved and that you have not left and rejoined the Discord server.

Open an application support ticket if your role has not been restored.

I cannot access a department +

Department access may require specific Discord roles, training completion or character permissions.

Contact the relevant department leadership before opening a general staff ticket.

A purchased package has not appeared +

Allow time for the purchase to process and confirm that the correct FiveM or CFX account was used.

Open a purchase support ticket with the transaction reference. Do not post receipts publicly.

How do I appeal a ban? +

Join the Union Roleplay Discord and use the designated sanction appeal ticket category.

Be honest, explain what happened and avoid blaming others or arguing with staff.

SUPPORT CONDUCT

Ticket Rules

Support tickets are treated as formal contact with the Union Roleplay staff team.

  • Do not open duplicate tickets for the same issue.
  • Do not tag individual staff members unless asked.
  • Do not insult, threaten or pressure support staff.
  • Do not submit edited or misleading evidence.
  • Keep confidential ticket information private.
  • Follow staff instructions while an issue is investigated.
