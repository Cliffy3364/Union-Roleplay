ABOUT UNION ROLEPLAY

More Than Just Another FiveM Server

Union Roleplay was created with one goal in mind: build a professional British roleplay community where quality always comes before quantity.

OUR STORY

Built By Players, For Players

Union Roleplay is a British QBOX economy server focused on immersive, realistic and enjoyable roleplay.

Every system has been designed to encourage meaningful interactions between players rather than repetitive grinding.

Whether you're protecting the city as a police officer, saving lives with Union Hospital Service, building a successful business, or creating your own criminal organisation, every action helps shape the city's future.
