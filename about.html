WELCOME TO UNION

A serious city built around your story

Union Roleplay is a British FiveM community designed for players who want believable characters, meaningful progression and roleplay that develops naturally over time.

From serving the public and building a business to leading an organisation or operating in the criminal underworld, every path contributes to the same living city. Your decisions create consequences, relationships and stories that continue beyond a single session.

OUR STORY

Built By Players, For Players

Union Roleplay was created because we wanted more than a server filled with repetitive jobs and short-lived scenes. We wanted a living city where choices have consequences, careers feel worthwhile and roleplay develops naturally over time.

Our systems are designed to support interaction. Police investigations create stories for officers and criminals. Union Health Service provides detailed medical roleplay. Player-owned businesses create employment, competition and reasons for citizens to meet.

Whether you join an emergency service, build a company, form a criminal organisation or live as an everyday citizen, your actions help shape the economy, relationships and future of Union City.

Your character. Your story. Your city. British roleplay with purpose.
