Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Information we process

When you sign in using Discord, we may process your Discord user ID, username, avatar and server roles. We also process information you submit through applications, support tickets and account preferences.

How information is used

Information is used to operate the whitelist, application and support systems; protect the community; manage permissions; and communicate decisions or updates.

Retention

Completed applications may be automatically deleted 48 hours after acceptance or denial. Staff may permanently delete completed applications sooner where appropriate. Support and audit records may be retained for moderation, safeguarding and operational purposes.

Sharing

Information is accessible only to authorised Union Roleplay staff and service providers required to operate the website. We do not sell personal information.

Your choices

You may contact Union Roleplay through Discord or the support system to ask about your information or request correction or deletion, subject to legitimate moderation and legal requirements.
