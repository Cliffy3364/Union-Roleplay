What Does Whitelisted Mean?

Union Roleplay is a whitelisted community. This means every player must complete an application before receiving access to the server.

The process helps us maintain a mature environment where players understand the rules, value their character's life and focus on creating enjoyable stories.

You do not need years of roleplay experience. We are looking for applicants who are willing to learn, communicate respectfully and contribute positively to the community.

COMMON QUESTIONS

Whitelist FAQ

How long will my application take? +

Most applications are reviewed within 72 hours. Review times may increase when a large number of applications have been submitted.

What happens if my application is declined? +

You may be given feedback and a date when you are permitted to apply again. Do not submit another application before that date.

Do I need previous FiveM experience? +

No. New roleplayers are welcome as long as they understand the rules, communicate respectfully and are willing to learn.

Will everyone need an interview? +

No. Interviews are only required when staff need more information or want to discuss part of an application.

Can I edit my application after submitting? +

Once the application system is fully connected, submitted applications will be available through your profile. Until then, contact staff through Discord if something important needs correcting.
