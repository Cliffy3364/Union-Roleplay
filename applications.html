STATUS GUIDE

What each status means

Application statuses may change as your submission moves through the review process.

Draft

Not submitted

Your answers are saved, but staff cannot review the application until you submit it.

Submitted

Waiting to be assigned

Your application has been received and is waiting for a member of the relevant team.

In review

Being reviewed

A staff member is currently checking your answers and account information.

Interview

Further assessment required

You need to complete an interview, scenario or practical assessment.

Approved

Application successful

Your application has passed and onboarding instructions will be provided.

Declined

Application unsuccessful

Your submission did not meet the current requirements. Review any feedback before applying again.

01

Answer honestly

Applications are reviewed for consistency. Honest, detailed answers are more valuable than exaggerated experience.

02

Avoid chasing reviews

Review times vary depending on the department and the number of active applications.

03

Check Discord regularly

Interview invitations and requests for additional information may be sent through Discord.

04

Read feedback carefully

Declined applications may include guidance to help you improve before submitting again.
