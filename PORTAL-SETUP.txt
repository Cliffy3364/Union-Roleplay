Current behaviour:

- Staff panel reads and updates those records on the same browser/device.
- Discord messages are prepared but disabled.

Later Discord setup:
1. Open js/portal-config.js
2. Paste your Discord webhook URL
3. Set discordEnabled to true

Important: GitHub Pages alone cannot provide secure shared staff data or authentication. A backend/database will be needed for production use across different devices.
