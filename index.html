Our roleplay philosophy

Story before outcome

Good roleplay means giving other people something to work with. Characters should have motives, weaknesses and relationships. Scenes should develop naturally and leave room for every participant to contribute.

We expect players to separate themselves from their characters, accept realistic consequences and use proper support routes when something goes wrong. Staff exist to protect the experience, not to decide who “won” a scene.

What you can become

Your path is yours

Start with an ordinary job, train for emergency services, establish a business, build a criminal network, work in media or become known simply through excellent character roleplay.

Union City is not built around one job or faction. It is an interconnected environment where actions affect reputations, relationships and opportunities across the server.
